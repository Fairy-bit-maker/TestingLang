Academic Excellence

Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Lipa (KLL) offers a variety of academic programs, including bachelor's degrees, associate degrees, and secondary and elementary education programs.

Our dedicated faculty employs innovative teaching methods to engage students and foster a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom.

Bachelor's Degree

Bachelor of Secondary Education: Includes BSED Filipino, BSED Values Education, BSED Social Studies, and BSED Mathematics

Bachelor of Elementary Education: Includes BEM 103 and LIT 1

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science: Includes BSCS

Bachelor of Science in Communication Arts: Includes RC3

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration: Includes BSBA major in Marketing Management

Bachelor of Science in Criminology: Includes BC Criminology

Associate degrees

Associate in Computer Technology: An exclusive offering at KLL

Secondary and elementary education programs

Includes BSED Filipino, ENG101, and PEC 113
